WhoreHole
What is WhoreHole?
WhoreHole is a 5 day, 4 night event in the Icelandic winter wilderness run by @actualwebutante. We will bask under the northern lights in a heated pool and soak away shame. The sauna and secret apartment onsite will provide ample space for cozy chats, and some limited workshops provided by the Whore herself will promote body positivity and self image. Expect ample discussions about pressures from society about self and gender.
When/Where is it?
Mid-January, 2025 in Siglufjörður, Iceland. The perfect time and place to see the aurora!
What events will happen?
The Whore herself will run some events, but like other vibe-based events, most of it will be up to you, the attendees! Intentions include:
  • Body Positivity Workshops
  • Peace and Serenity
  • Warmth and support
  • Not an orgy, but there will be sensuality
How much are tickets?
There are 3 tiers of tickets. Each one covers lodging and food for the entire event as well as transit to and from Reykjavík. No other transit is covered.
  • Basic: $500.
  • Fund-a-whore: $750. If you can afford it, each ticket at this price pays for a ticket at the subsidized price for lower-income attendees. Rember the ancient words: "it is the noblesse obligee of the tech bro to bring to fruition the creativity of the art hoe."
  • Poor Whore: $250. Intended for lower income members who could not make it otherwise. Limited supply based on the purchase of fund-a-whore tickets.
